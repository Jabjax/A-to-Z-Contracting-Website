    Our Mission

    A to Z was founded in 1987 by Jeff Whiteside, who worked for many years either by himself or with one other man. His tireless work over the many years lead to where we are now, as a premier waterproofing company in the Detroit area. Our goal here at A to Z is to deliver the highest quality job to our Detroit area customers. We warranty our work for anywhere from 10 years to a lifetime, we believe this shows our commitment to quality craftsmanship, and doing the job right the first time.

    Main Services

    Foundation

    Have a basement wall starting to crumble or fall into your home? Get a quote from us, and we can straighten your problem right out. This problem can occur in many old homes, and all the way from full wall replacement to simple crack repair, we can do it all.

    Waterproofing

    Have a basement that fills with water? Making your home smell damp and musty? Get a quote from us, waterproofing is our specialty! We offer indoor and outdoor waterproofing systems in accordance with your budget and water problems. Jobs will have between a 10 to lifetime warranty.

    Main Line Repair

    Have water or sewage that just doesn't flow right? But, you have tried everything with no results? Your main lines could be damaged underground! Give us a call and get a quote to see what we can do for you! In most cases we can have your utilities back up and running properly in a matter of days.

    Side Services
